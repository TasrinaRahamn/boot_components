Flowers Details

After years of working for a large floral distribution company out of Miami, Corrine Heck felt inspired to step away from flower importing, and open her own design studio in Ormond Beach, Florida. Corrine's passion has always been design, so opening a wedding flowers studio was a dream come true. Although the idea of opening a flower business was new to her-- there simply wasn't a "handbook" or network to refer to. Corrine began crafting technology to make her tedious job of calculating, drafting, and procuring easier for her new flower business.
